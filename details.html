Perched atop 432 Park Avenue, the Obsidian Penthouse represents the absolute pinnacle of New York luxury living. An architectural tour de force spanning three floors and 12,400 square feet, this residence was conceived by Pritzker Prize laureate Rafael Viñoly and completed in 2022 with a total fit-out budget exceeding $12 million.

Floor-to-ceiling windows frame uninterrupted 360° panoramas of Manhattan, the Hudson River, and on clear days, the horizon beyond. Hand-selected Carrara marble, custom-milled walnut millwork, and a Crestron smart-home system woven invisibly into the architecture create an atmosphere of effortless mastery.
